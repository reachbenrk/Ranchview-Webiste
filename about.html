About us

At Ranchview High School we offer strong well rooted academic programs designed to help every student grow, learn, and prepare for the future.

Our history

Ranchview High School was opened in the early 2000s as part of the Carrollton Farmers Branch Independent School District, created to serve the rapidly growing communities of Irving and Valley Ranch. From the beginning, the school was designed with a forwardthinking vision of being the school that feels like home to every student and also one in which everyone could depend on.
